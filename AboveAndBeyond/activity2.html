Activity 2:

For the scenario of a Smart-Building:

  • What does smart mean?
  • Think of some smart-building scenarios where this well be beneficial.
  • What are the parts needed for it to work?

As if you are in the group or as an individual if you are online try to answer these questions by:

  • Create lists.
  • Create a mind map.
  • Create a mini-presentation to explain to others.

Ans1

Ans1: Smart means to do the work with a more clear mind to get best ideas in the least time possible to work fastly in order to minimize the time and efforts required for the same task with higher efficiency.
Ans2: The smart building scenario concept will be beneficial in places where one can interact with mulitiple people and perform the tasks required for them in least possible time, with the usage of the same system in the same environment.
Ans3: The main parts are the features needed for such a scenario such as
  • Better algorithms
  • Good idea
  • Less complex solution
  • Actuators
  • Time management
  • Reduced expenses
